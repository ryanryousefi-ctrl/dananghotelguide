Wyndham Soleil Danang Review

From $85/night ★ 8.4 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

Wyndham Soleil Danang is a 34-floor beachfront hotel on My Khe Beach, operating under Wyndham Hotels & Resorts' upper-midscale banner. With 322 rooms and an emphasis on rooftop facilities, it caters to leisure travellers who want beach access, Wyndham Rewards points, and a contemporary hotel without five-star resort pricing.

The Soleil concept within Wyndham is styled for lifestyle travellers — the design is brighter and more contemporary than older Wyndham properties, with public spaces designed for social interaction rather than purely transactional hotel use. The rooftop pool and bar are the hotel's most photographed features.

Location

My Khe Beach position with 6 km to Dragon Bridge, 8 km to the airport. Local restaurants on the beach road are walkable. Hoi An is 30 minutes south.

  • Airport: 8 km / 12 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 14 min taxi
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car
  • Beach restaurants: 5 min walk

Rooms and Design

322 rooms across 34 floors. Contemporary design with floor-to-ceiling windows and ocean or city views on higher floors. Entry rooms run around 35–38 square metres. Ocean-view rooms from the 20th floor upward are particularly strong. The design is clean and modern, though not distinctive enough to be a selling point in itself.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

The rooftop pool on the upper floors is the hotel's signature feature, offering elevated views over My Khe Beach. It is a highlight compared to the ground-level or mid-building pools at many competitors. Direct beach access is managed via the hotel's beach service area on My Khe sand. A fitness centre and spa are available.

Dining Options

The rooftop Sky Bar is a cocktail venue and light dining space adjacent to the pool with panoramic views. The main ground-floor restaurant serves buffet breakfast and all-day dining. Room service is available throughout the day. Local restaurants are a short walk away for dinner.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $85–$120/night
  • Shoulder season: $120–$175/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $160–$250/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

Wyndham Rewards members accumulating points, couples and younger travellers who appreciate the social rooftop environment, and guests who want My Khe Beach access without five-star pricing. Also works well for those mixing city exploration with beach time.

Pros
  • Rooftop pool with panoramic beach views
  • Wyndham Rewards points earned
  • Direct My Khe Beach access
  • Contemporary design with good upper-floor views
  • Competitive rates for a beachfront position
Cons
  • Rooms on smaller side compared to resort competitors
  • Pool can get crowded on weekends
  • Service less polished than five-star neighbours
  • Limited spa compared to resort properties

Final Verdict

Wyndham Soleil Danang is a competent and well-positioned beachfront hotel that delivers reliable quality for its price bracket. The rooftop pool is a genuine asset, the beach access is direct, and Wyndham Rewards members get solid points value. It is not a resort and not trying to be one — but for a beach holiday with urban convenience, it performs well.

Is the Wyndham Soleil Danang Worth It?

The Wyndham Soleil Danang is one of the city's newer hotels and benefits from fresh design and modern room specifications. The central My Khe Beach location is strong, the pool is well-done, and Wyndham Rewards members will find useful points-earning at a beach hotel with relatively few Wyndham brand options in Da Nang. The honest assessment is that it sits in a crowded midscale-to-upscale beach hotel category where competition is fierce — it needs to be compared against the Four Points by Sheraton, TMS Hotel, and A La Carte at similar price points. It competes well on room quality and modernity. Where it is less differentiated is in F&B and overall resort depth. For most travellers, it represents solid value for a new-ish beachfront stay in Da Nang.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Wyndham Soleil Danang, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — On-site. Direct beachfront access on Da Nang's main coastal strip.
  • Dragon Bridge — 10 min taxi. A short taxi ride to Da Nang's most iconic attraction.
  • Han River Waterfront — 12 min taxi. Riverside restaurants and the rotating Love Bridge.
  • My Khe Restaurants — 2 min walk. Seafood grills and local restaurants immediately outside.
  • Da Nang Airport — 8 km / 12 min taxi. Practical for arrivals and departures.
  • Marble Mountains — 20 min taxi south. Classic half-day excursion to the five marble hills.
