Grand Mercure Da Nang Review

From $70/night ★ 8.2 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

The Grand Mercure Da Nang sits in the city centre, offering a reliable four-star city hotel experience rather than a beach resort. Operated by Accor under the Grand Mercure banner — which positions between Novotel and Sofitel — it provides well-designed rooms, a central location near the Han River, and the Accor ALL loyalty programme for points earners.

The hotel caters to a wide mix: business travellers attending meetings in the commercial district, leisure visitors using Da Nang as a base for exploring the surrounding region, and transit travellers taking advantage of the 3 km airport proximity. It is consistently well-reviewed for cleanliness, service friendliness, and location convenience.

Location

Located in central Da Nang close to the Han River. Dragon Bridge is 5 minutes' walk and My Khe Beach is a 10-minute taxi ride. The airport proximity — just 3 km — makes it one of the fastest hotel check-ins in the city from the terminal. Han Market, Da Nang Cathedral, and the local restaurant streets are all walkable.

  • Airport: 3 km / 8 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 5 min walk
  • My Khe Beach: 10 min taxi
  • Han Market: 10 min walk
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car

Rooms and Design

Rooms run 32–45 square metres. Design follows Grand Mercure's contemporary regional aesthetic — local art and craft elements incorporated into a modern framework. Upper floors offer Han River and city views. The executive floor rooms add lounge access with complimentary breakfast and evening canapes.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

A rooftop pool with city views is available. Standard four-star facilities including a fitness centre, spa, and business centre. No beach access — this is a city hotel.

Dining Options

The main restaurant serves a solid international buffet breakfast and Vietnamese à la carte. A lobby bar and rooftop bar are available. The hotel's walking distance from Da Nang's restaurant streets means most guests eat out for at least some meals.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $70–$100/night
  • Shoulder season: $95–$145/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $115–$185/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

Business travellers, city explorers, and Accor ALL loyalty members who want a convenient, reliable base close to Da Nang's landmarks and the airport.

Pros
  • 3 km from airport — fastest major hotel transfer in Da Nang
  • Walking distance to Dragon Bridge and Han Market
  • Accor ALL loyalty points earned
  • Competitive rates for a 4-star city hotel
  • Clean, well-maintained rooms with good service
Cons
  • 4 km from My Khe Beach — requires taxi for beach visits
  • No resort atmosphere
  • Pool modest compared to beachfront hotels
  • Surrounding area can be noisy in the evening

Final Verdict

The Grand Mercure Da Nang is a dependable, well-priced city hotel with the right location for urban exploration. It won't satisfy guests seeking a beach resort experience, but for travellers whose itinerary includes food markets, cultural sites, and day trips to Hoi An, it is a smart and cost-effective choice.

Is the Grand Mercure Danang Worth It?

The Grand Mercure Danang is Da Nang's best city-centre hotel option for travellers who want genuine luxury in a non-beach location. Its Han River position gives it views that rival far more expensive properties, and the facilities — rooftop pool, spa, multiple restaurants — are strong for a city hotel at this price point. Compared to the Novotel Han River, it offers a step up in room quality and design. Compared to My Khe Beach resorts, the trade-off is no direct beach access, replaced by urban convenience and city views. For business travellers, cultural visitors, and those who want to be walking distance from Da Nang's restaurants and markets, it is excellent value and one of the most under-appreciated hotels in the city.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Grand Mercure Danang, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • Han River Waterfront — On-site. Directly on the river — Dragon Bridge and city views from most rooms.
  • Dragon Bridge — 5 min walk. The city's most iconic landmark is an easy stroll from the hotel.
  • Han Market — 10 min walk. Da Nang's main market for local produce, street food, and textiles.
  • Da Nang Cathedral — 10 min walk. The famous pink cathedral — a city landmark.
  • My Khe Beach — 10 min taxi. The main beach is easily accessible by taxi.
  • Hoi An Ancient Town — 30 min by car. Easy day trip — the hotel's concierge can arrange transfers.
