TMS Hotel Da Nang Beach Review

Last reviewed: March 2026

From $70/night ★ 8.3 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

TMS Hotel Da Nang Beach is a locally operated four-star hotel that has built a strong reputation on My Khe Beach for delivering reliable quality at competitive rates. It is not a luxury resort — the lobby is functional, the design is conventional — but for travellers who primarily want a clean room, a pool, and direct access to one of Vietnam's best beaches at a fair price, TMS consistently performs well.

The hotel has 155 rooms across 20 floors, with the upper floors offering the best ocean views. It caters primarily to Vietnamese domestic tourists, Korean visitors, and international budget-to-midscale travellers. The breakfast is widely praised, and the beachfront location is genuine rather than 'beach access via a road crossing'.

Location

The My Khe Beach location is central and convenient. Dragon Bridge is 6 km to the northwest and Han Market 8 km away. The airport is 8 km. The local restaurant strip — known for fresh seafood grills — runs along the beach road in both directions from the hotel. This walkability to affordable local restaurants makes TMS a good choice for food-focused travellers on a moderate budget.

  • Airport: 8 km / 12 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 12 min taxi
  • Local seafood restaurants: 2–5 min walk
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car

Rooms and Design

Rooms are in the 30–38 square metre range. Ocean-view rooms on the upper floors are the most popular, delivering views worth the slight premium. Design is functional contemporary without distinctive character — clean white finishes, standard hospitality furniture. Beds are comfortable and linens well-maintained.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

An outdoor pool sits between the building and the beach. A beach service area provides umbrellas and loungers for guests on the My Khe sand directly in front. A basic spa and fitness room are available.

Dining Options

The breakfast buffet is included in most rate packages and is regularly praised for quality and variety — a strong Vietnamese and international spread. A rooftop bar offers cocktails and light snacks with good beach views. On-site dinner options are adequate but the local restaurant strip is more worthwhile for dinner.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $70–$100/night
  • Shoulder season: $95–$145/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $120–$195/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

Mid-budget travellers who want genuine My Khe Beach access without paying five-star rates, families seeking a clean beachfront base, and domestic Vietnamese travellers familiar with the TMS brand. It is also a solid choice for solo travellers who prioritise location and value.

Pros
  • Genuine beachfront access at one of the best prices on My Khe
  • Breakfast consistently praised — included in most rates
  • Upper-floor ocean views are excellent for the price
  • Walkable to affordable local seafood restaurants
  • Clean and well-maintained throughout
Cons
  • Design lacks character compared to boutique alternatives
  • Pool small compared to resort competitors
  • No upscale spa or extensive wellness facilities
  • Lobby and public areas feel dated

Final Verdict

TMS Hotel Da Nang Beach does not try to be what it is not. It is a well-run, value-oriented beachfront hotel that delivers on the basics — clean rooms, a good breakfast, and genuine My Khe Beach access. For travellers who don't need resort-scale facilities and would rather put the savings toward local restaurants and Hoi An day trips, it represents one of the best price-to-location ratios on the beach strip.

Is TMS Hotel Danang Beach Worth It?

TMS Hotel Danang Beach is one of the best value propositions on My Khe Beach — it delivers direct beachfront access, a decent pool, and consistent mid-range comfort at rates that undercut most international branded properties by a meaningful margin. It won't compete with the Hyatt Regency or Sheraton Grand on room quality or facilities depth, and that is not the point. The point is: clean, well-located beachfront accommodation with good service, a useful family setup, and walking distance to the My Khe restaurant strip. For budget-to-midscale travellers who want to be on the beach without paying resort hotel rates, TMS is a reliable and consistently recommended choice.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From TMS Hotel Danang Beach, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — On-site. Direct beachfront — one of Da Nang's best urban beach stretches.
  • My Khe Restaurant Strip — 2 min walk. Dozens of fresh seafood restaurants immediately adjacent.
  • Dragon Bridge — 10 min taxi. Da Nang's most photographed attraction.
  • Han Market — 15 min taxi. The city's main covered market.
  • Da Nang Airport — 8 km / 12 min taxi. Convenient for arrivals and departures.
  • Marble Mountains — 20 min taxi south. Five marble hills with temples and caves.
