Hilton Da Nang Review

From $130/night ★ 8.6 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

The Hilton Da Nang occupies a prime position on My Khe Beach — the main coastal strip that runs through Da Nang's east side. As one of the city's most recognisable international brand hotels, it appeals to travellers who want the reassurance of a known chain alongside genuine beachfront access. The 22-floor tower opened in 2015 and has 252 rooms, a rooftop pool, multiple dining outlets, and an elevated spa.

It occupies a sweet spot in the Da Nang market: upscale enough to deliver a polished experience, but positioned at rates that undercut the resort properties to the south. The location on the My Khe strip means guests can walk to dozens of seafood restaurants, bars, and cafes within minutes — a significant advantage over more isolated resorts.

Location

My Khe Beach is Da Nang's primary urban beach — a broad, clean stretch of white sand running south from Son Tra Peninsula. The Hilton sits at the northern end of the busy hotel strip, roughly 6 km from Dragon Bridge and 4 km from Han Market. Da Nang International Airport is 8 km away, making the journey one of the quickest airport-to-hotel transfers of any quality hotel in the city.

The beach directly in front of the hotel is wide and well-maintained. Unlike the more secluded resort beaches further south, My Khe here has a lively, accessible feel — local swimmers, tourist sunbathers, and beach cafes all share the shoreline. This energy is a plus for travellers who want to feel connected to the city, and a minus for those seeking tranquillity.

  • Airport: 8 km / 12 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 10 min taxi
  • Han Market: 15 min taxi
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car
  • Local restaurants: 2–5 min walk

Rooms and Design

The 252 rooms and suites lean into a clean, contemporary design with warm tones and floor-to-ceiling windows that maximise the ocean views. Superior rooms start at around 38 square metres — compact by luxury resort standards but well-planned and efficiently designed. The upper-floor Deluxe Ocean View rooms are the best value upgrade, adding meaningful panoramas over My Khe Beach without a dramatic rate jump.

Executive floors include access to the Executive Lounge, which provides complimentary breakfast, evening cocktails, and a quieter working environment — popular with business travellers. Suites on the uppermost floors are genuinely impressive, with wraparound balconies and long views up the coast toward Son Tra.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

The rooftop infinity pool on the 22nd floor is a genuine highlight — with views across My Khe Beach, Son Tra Peninsula, and on clear days toward the Hai Van Pass. It is open to all guests and tends to be less crowded than ground-level hotel pools. A second pool is available for families on a lower level.

The Da Nang Spa & Fitness Centre is a full-service spa with eight treatment rooms and a well-equipped gym. The hotel has a small children's pool and offers beach umbrellas and loungers directly on My Khe. Bicycle rental and a business centre are also available.

Dining Options

Ren Chinese Restaurant is the most distinctive dining option on-site, serving Cantonese cuisine in a refined setting — popular with both hotel guests and outside diners. The Gallery Lounge is the main all-day dining venue, serving a substantial breakfast buffet. Edge Bar occupies the rooftop adjacent to the pool and is popular for sunset cocktails.

The My Khe Beach restaurant strip begins almost directly outside the hotel, offering dozens of seafood grills, Vietnamese noodle shops, and international cafes within easy walking distance. This proximity to good external dining is one of the Hilton's genuine location advantages over more isolated resort properties.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $130–$175/night
  • Shoulder season: $175–$240/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $220–$340/night
  • Suites: from $280/night in low season

Who This Hotel Is Best For

The Hilton Da Nang works well for business travellers who need reliable infrastructure and easy airport access, couples wanting beachfront convenience without full resort seclusion, and Hilton Honors loyalty members building points. It is a strong choice for those who want to split time between beach relaxation and exploring Da Nang's food and cultural scene, given its walkable location.

Pros
  • Genuine My Khe Beach frontage with in-hotel beach access
  • Rooftop infinity pool with panoramic city and sea views
  • 8 km from airport — one of the fastest transfers in Da Nang
  • Walking distance to dozens of restaurants and cafes
  • Hilton Honors points earned
  • Executive Lounge adds strong value for business guests
Cons
  • Rooms smaller than comparable resort properties
  • My Khe Beach strip can be noisy during peak season
  • Pool complex less extensive than resort competitors
  • In-room decor shows some age in standard rooms

Final Verdict

The Hilton Da Nang is a strong performer in its market segment — a reliable, well-located beachfront hotel with a memorable rooftop pool and the convenience of the My Khe strip on its doorstep. It is not a resort in the traditional sense and doesn't pretend to be: this is a city beach hotel that delivers urban convenience alongside direct beach access. For travellers who want to explore Da Nang properly while having a quality base, it is one of the best options on the beach.

Is the Hilton Da Nang Worth Staying At?

For most travellers visiting Da Nang, yes. The Hilton strikes one of the best balances in the city between beach access, location convenience, and brand reliability. The rooftop pool is a genuine highlight, the airport transfer is the fastest of any quality hotel in Da Nang at just 8 km, and the My Khe Beach strip directly outside the hotel gives guests immediate access to some of the best seafood restaurants in Vietnam. It is not a resort and should not be booked as one — there is no sprawling pool complex or jungle spa. But as a city beach hotel that allows you to explore Da Nang properly while sleeping next to the ocean, it is hard to beat at its price point.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Hilton Da Nang, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — On-site. Direct beach access — one of the best urban beaches in Southeast Asia.
  • Dragon Bridge — 10 min taxi. Da Nang's most iconic landmark — fire-breathing show on Friday and Saturday nights.
  • Han Market — 15 min taxi. The city's main covered market for local produce, textiles, and street food.
  • Han River Waterfront — 12 min taxi. Riverside promenade with restaurants, the Swing Bridge, and night views.
  • Son Tra Peninsula — 20 min taxi north. Forested headland with the Lady Buddha statue and ocean viewpoints.
  • Marble Mountains — 20 min taxi south. Five limestone hills with ancient temples and caves.
