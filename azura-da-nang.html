Azura Da Nang Hotel Review

Last reviewed: March 2026

From $50/night ★ 8.6 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

Azura Da Nang Hotel is a boutique property on the Han River that punches well above its price point in terms of location quality and design character. With 36 rooms across 16 floors, it offers an intimacy rare in Da Nang's hotel market — attentive service, personalised attention, and a rooftop pool with remarkable Dragon Bridge views that would be impressive at three times the rate.

The hotel is popular with independent travellers, digital nomads, and couples who value design and location over resort-scale facilities. Its rating on Booking.com consistently exceeds larger hotels charging higher rates — a reflection of the value it delivers.

Location

A prime Han River position close to Dragon Bridge and the main restaurant strip. Everything of interest in central Da Nang is walkable: Han Market, Da Nang Cathedral, the riverside promenade, and dozens of cafes and restaurants. My Khe Beach requires a 10-minute taxi.

  • Airport: 4 km / 8 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 3 min walk
  • Han Market: 8 min walk
  • My Khe Beach: 10 min taxi
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car

Rooms and Design

36 rooms are compact but thoughtfully designed with quality finishes. River-view rooms facing Dragon Bridge are the standout category. The design aesthetic is contemporary with local craft elements — an upgrade from the generic business-hotel look of comparable budget properties. Breakfast is included in most rates.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

A small rooftop pool sits beneath spectacular Dragon Bridge views. The pool is tiny — this is not a swimming pool but a dipping pool for cooling down — but the views compensate decisively. A rooftop bar operates alongside it. Fitness facilities are limited.

Dining Options

Breakfast is included and well-reviewed. A rooftop bar serves cocktails and light bites. The surrounding streets offer some of Da Nang's best independent restaurant options.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $50–$75/night (incl. breakfast)
  • Shoulder season: $70–$105/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $90–$155/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

Independent travellers, digital nomads, solo visitors, and couples seeking a boutique experience at budget-friendly rates. Perfect for those who want to explore Da Nang and Hoi An and treat the hotel as a central base.

Pros
  • Exceptional location adjacent to Dragon Bridge
  • Rooftop pool with one of Da Nang's best views at any price
  • Boutique scale means genuinely personal service
  • Breakfast included
  • Very competitive rates for the location quality
Cons
  • Compact rooms — not suitable for those needing space
  • Pool tiny — a dip pool, not a swimming pool
  • Limited fitness and spa facilities
  • Can book out early during peak season at these rates

Final Verdict

Azura Da Nang Hotel is a compelling boutique option for travellers who prioritise location and character over resort facilities. At its price point, the Han River position and Dragon Bridge views represent outstanding value — and the rooftop pool view is one that travellers remember long after leaving.

Is Azura Da Nang Hotel Worth It?

Azura Da Nang is a boutique hotel option that appeals to travellers who want something more personal and characterful than the large resort properties. The design is contemporary Vietnamese with a boutique aesthetic, the service is more attentive at this smaller scale, and the location gives good access to both My Khe Beach and the city centre. At its price point, it competes favourably with the lower end of the international brand hotels. The gaps are in facilities — no spa, limited pool — but for travellers who spend their days exploring Da Nang rather than staying poolside, those limitations matter less. A strong option for independent travellers and couples who prefer boutique character over resort infrastructure.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Azura Da Nang Hotel, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — 10 min walk or 5 min taxi. Da Nang's main beach — within easy reach of the hotel.
  • Dragon Bridge — 10 min taxi. Da Nang's most famous landmark.
  • Han River — 10 min taxi. City-centre riverside dining and nightlife.
  • Han Market — 12 min taxi. Da Nang's main covered market.
  • Da Nang Cathedral — 10 min taxi. The famous pink church in the city centre.
  • My Khe Restaurants — 10 min walk. Seafood restaurants and street food on the beachfront road.
