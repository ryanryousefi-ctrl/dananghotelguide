Overview

Da Nang's Beachfront Scene

Da Nang's 30km coastline divided into three distinct hotel zones — North and Central My Khe, South My Khe luxury resorts, and the ultraluxury Non Nuoc and Son Tra Peninsula

Da Nang has earned its place among Southeast Asia's best beach destinations not by accident but because of a rare combination: genuine five-star infrastructure, a city large enough to eat and explore well, and 30 kilometres of South China Sea coastline that ranges from urban beach strip to secluded private bay. Few cities in the region can match this combination at comparable price points.

My Khe Beach is the main hotel corridor — a 10km stretch of white sand with the densest concentration of accommodation in the city. The northern My Khe zone (around Vo Nguyen Giap and Ho Xuan Huong streets) is quieter and less developed, popular with travellers wanting reasonable rates near the sand. Central My Khe, anchored by the An Thuong neighbourhood, is Da Nang's tourist heart: boutique hotels, Vietnamese restaurants, beach cafes, and direct sand access within walking distance. Southern My Khe is where the large luxury resorts begin — the Pullman, Furama, and Hyatt Regency anchoring a stretch that increasingly resembles a proper beach resort destination.

Non Nuoc Beach, south of the Marble Mountains, is a different world entirely. The resort compounds here — Hyatt Regency, Premier Village, Marriott — operate almost as self-contained villages, with their own restaurants, pools, kids' clubs, and beach clubs. The surf is calmer here than at My Khe, making it the right choice for families with young children. Hoi An is 35 minutes south.

Son Tra Peninsula is where Da Nang's most exclusive address sits: the InterContinental Da Nang Sun Peninsula Resort, perched above a private bay with no neighbouring properties and direct access to the cleanest water in the region. This is the choice for travellers to whom money is secondary to experience.

📍 Zone tip

My Khe and Non Nuoc are 20 minutes apart by taxi. If you're unsure which zone suits you, book My Khe for access and flexibility — you can always make a day trip to Non Nuoc. Families and resort-focused travellers should strongly consider Non Nuoc or South My Khe for the larger compound experience.

Zone 1

North My Khe Beach Hotels

The quieter, less-developed northern end of My Khe Beach. Hotels here sit on a calmer stretch of coastline with fewer beach vendors and more space. Slightly removed from the An Thuong restaurant cluster, but well-connected by taxi. Good value relative to central My Khe.

North My Khe — quieter, less commercialised, and 20–30% cheaper than the central tourist strip

Zone 2

Zone 3

South My Khe Luxury Resorts

The resort corridor. Properties here operate as full-compound luxury destinations — multiple pools, private beaches, beach clubs, multiple restaurants, spas, and kids' programmes. These aren't just hotels with beach access; they're self-sufficient resorts where guests may spend their entire stay on property.

South My Khe luxury corridor — where Da Nang's beach hotel market transitions from city hotel to full resort

Zone 4

Non Nuoc & Son Tra Ultra-Luxury

The highest tier of Da Nang's beach hotel market. Non Nuoc is quieter and wider than My Khe, with calmer surf that's ideal for families. Son Tra Peninsula is where Da Nang's most exclusive address sits — the InterContinental, perched above a private sheltered bay with no neighbours. Properties here start from around $180/night and reach $500+.

🏆 Monsoon season note

Da Nang's northeast monsoon (September–November) makes open-sea swimming difficult. The InterContinental's sheltered private bay on Son Tra remains swimmable year-round — making it specifically worth considering if your trip overlaps this window. All other Non Nuoc and My Khe properties redirect guests to pools during high-swell periods.

Booking Guide

Beach Hotel Booking Tips

Book 6–8 weeks ahead for July–August. Da Nang's peak beach season coincides with Vietnamese school holidays and significant domestic tourism. Beachfront properties on My Khe sell out weeks in advance during this window, and rates spike 40–60% above shoulder-season levels. The Non Nuoc resorts have more inventory but still fill up for the July peak.

March to June is the best value window. The beach is at its best — calm water, consistent sunshine, low rainfall — and rates haven't reached peak-season levels. This is when the best properties are accessible at reasonable prices. See the seasonal guide for monthly detail.

My Khe vs Non Nuoc — the honest comparison. My Khe wins on convenience, dining variety, and urban access. Non Nuoc wins on space, calmer water, and resort scale. For most first-time visitors, central My Khe is the right default. For families with young children, Non Nuoc is worth the extra 15-minute drive. For ultra-luxury, Son Tra is in a different category entirely. Also see our Where to Stay in Da Nang guide for a full neighbourhood comparison.

The "one block back" calculation. Hotels directly on My Khe command a 30–45% premium over properties one street back. In practice, the walk to the sand from a street-behind property is 60–90 seconds. For budget and mid-range travellers, the price gap rarely justifies the beachfront premium — especially in the An Thuong zone where the street itself is a destination. For luxury travellers, the price gap narrows and the direct beach gate access becomes more meaningful.

FAQ

Beach Hotel FAQs

What is the best beachfront hotel in Da Nang?
For ultra-luxury, the InterContinental Da Nang Sun Peninsula Resort on Son Tra Peninsula is the clear standout — one of Asia's most acclaimed resort properties, with a private sheltered bay and exceptional design. For luxury on My Khe, the Sheraton Grand Danang or Pullman are the benchmark five-star properties. For design and value, Chicland Hotel in the central An Thuong zone is the best mid-range pick. For families, Hyatt Regency Da Nang on the southern stretch delivers the most complete resort package.
Is My Khe Beach or Non Nuoc Beach better?
It depends on your travel style. My Khe is better for urban access, restaurant variety, and convenience — the An Thuong village strip has some of Da Nang's best independent dining within walking distance. Non Nuoc is better for families (calmer water, larger resort compounds with kids' programmes), resort-focused stays, and travellers planning Hoi An day trips. Neither beach has a significant quality advantage for sand — the difference is atmosphere and surrounding infrastructure.
What is the best budget beach hotel in Da Nang?
The Hilton Garden Inn Da Nang consistently offers the best budget four-star beachfront value. Diamond Sea Hotel and Adamo are strong mid-range options in the central zone. For travellers prioritising location over brand, properties one block from My Khe (particularly in the An Thuong zone) offer the same beach access at 25–35% less than direct beachfront rates.
Can you swim at Da Nang beach year-round?
The main swimming season is March through August. September through November brings the northeast monsoon — seas can be rough and red flags are common. December through February has improving but variable conditions. The InterContinental Sun Peninsula's private bay on Son Tra is sheltered and swimmable year-round, making it specifically valuable for shoulder-season travellers.
How far are the Non Nuoc resorts from Da Nang city centre?
Non Nuoc Beach is approximately 13km south of Da Nang city centre — about 20–25 minutes by taxi or Grab. It's further than My Khe but not inconveniently so. Most guests staying in Non Nuoc use taxis for city trips; Grab is available throughout the route. Hoi An is a further 20 minutes south, making Non Nuoc the most logical base for travellers combining Da Nang city with a Hoi An visit.
Which Da Nang beach hotel is best for families?
The Hyatt Regency Da Nang Resort & Spa is the best family resort overall — six hectares, four pools, a dedicated kids' club, and the calmest surf on the southern stretch. For Non Nuoc, the Marriott Resort & Spa also has an excellent kids' programme. Premier Village's pool villa format works well for families wanting private outdoor space. For families on a tighter budget, the Pullman Danang Beach Resort offers the best full-resort family experience at a more accessible price.
