Sheraton Grand Danang Resort Review

✎ Written by Ryan Yousefi · 📅 Last updated: March 2026 · 5 stars · From $155/night

At a Glance

Da Nang's premier family resort with an extraordinary water complex, 785 rooms, and the best Club Lounge in the city. My An Beach frontage and dedicated kids areas make this the top choice for families.

Location & Setting

Located in My An Beach, Da Nang. The hotel occupies one of Da Nang's most sought-after positions — My An Beach is consistently ranked among the city's top locations for accommodation due to its balance of beach access, resort facilities, and proximity to the city's dining and transport network.

Da Nang International Airport is approximately 20 minutes by taxi. For transport options, see our Da Nang Transport Guide.

Rooms & Accommodation

Rates start from $155/night in mid-season. The hotel offers a range of room categories from standard rooms to suites and villas depending on availability and booking period. We recommend booking the sea-view or premium category for the best experience.

Check current rates and availability on Booking.com — live pricing reflects seasonal demand and is updated daily.

Facilities & Amenities

Sheraton Grand Danang Resort provides the facilities expected of a 5-star resort in Da Nang, including pool access, beach proximity (or direct beach access where applicable), dining options, and fitness facilities. Full amenity details are confirmed on the hotel's direct booking page.

Strengths

  • Strong location in My An Beach
  • Reliable service quality for category
  • Good beach or pool access
  • Competitive rates vs international peers

Considerations

  • Taxi required for city restaurants
  • Book early for peak season (Jul–Aug)
  • Sea-view rooms command a premium

Who It's Best For

Couples and honeymooners seeking luxury and seclusion. The hotel is particularly well-suited to travellers visiting Da Nang for 3–7 nights who want a base that provides genuine resort amenities without requiring constant off-resort excursions for dining or activities.
