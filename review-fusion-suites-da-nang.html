Fusion Suites Da Nang Review

From $110/night ★ 8.7 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

Fusion Suites Da Nang is part of the Fusion Hotels group, a Vietnam-based boutique hotel brand with a distinctive philosophy: all stays include daily spa treatments for every guest. This is not a promotional add-on — it is a core part of the brand's offer. Every night booked at Fusion Suites includes a set number of spa treatment credits, applied from the full treatment menu. Breakfast is also included as standard.

The property has 82 suites (no standard rooms — all accommodations are suites of at least 45 square metres) across a well-designed tower directly on My Khe Beach. The building has a rooftop pool, a full spa facility, a beachfront location, and a size that allows for genuinely personalised service. It is one of the most interesting boutique hotel concepts operating in Da Nang.

Location

The My Khe Beach location is central — Dragon Bridge is 5 km, the airport 7 km, and the local restaurant strip is walkable. This is a better urban position than the Fusion Maia Resort in Hoi An (which is more isolated), giving guests easy access to Da Nang's food and cultural scene alongside the resort amenities.

  • Airport: 7 km / 12 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 12 min taxi
  • Han Market: 18 min taxi
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car
  • Beach restaurants: 5 min walk

Rooms and Design

All 82 rooms are suites — the entry-level Deluxe Suite runs around 45 square metres, which is substantially larger than standard rooms at comparably priced competitors. Contemporary design with Vietnamese craft touches, large windows, and well-appointed bathrooms. Higher-floor ocean-view suites are the premium option and worth the upgrade for the views alone. Penthouse suites with private terrace are the pinnacle of the portfolio.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

A rooftop infinity pool with views over My Khe Beach is the primary pool facility — it is compact but elevated and atmospheric. The spa facility is central to the Fusion concept: multiple treatment rooms, steam room, relaxation areas. All guests receive daily spa treatment credits. Beach access is directly in front of the hotel.

Dining Options

Breakfast is included for all guests — a substantial spread served in the ground-floor restaurant. A rooftop bar offers cocktails and snacks adjacent to the pool. On-site dinner options are limited, but the local beach restaurant strip fills this gap effectively.

Typical Price Range

All rates include daily spa treatments and breakfast, which substantially improves the all-in value versus hotels at similar nominal rates.

  • Low season (May–August): $110–$155/night (incl. spa + breakfast)
  • Shoulder season: $155–$220/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $200–$310/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

Fusion Suites is particularly well-suited to couples, honeymooners, and solo female travellers who value the spa inclusion. The all-suite format works well for anyone who wants more space than a standard hotel room provides. It also appeals to travellers who want a boutique, independently-operated feel rather than an international chain environment.

Pros
  • Daily spa treatments genuinely included — exceptional value
  • All-suite format provides more space than competitors at same price
  • Breakfast included as standard
  • My Khe Beach direct access
  • Boutique scale allows for personalised service
  • Rooftop pool with good ocean views
Cons
  • Rooftop pool small compared to resort competitors
  • On-site dining limited beyond breakfast
  • Rooftop bar can get crowded in peak season
  • Not ideal for families with very young children

Final Verdict

Fusion Suites Da Nang delivers one of the most cleverly constructed value propositions in the city's hotel market. When you factor in the included spa treatments and breakfast, the effective all-in daily cost compares very favourably to larger hotels charging equivalent headline rates for rooms half the size. For couples or spa-focused travellers, it is one of Da Nang's most recommended stays.

Is Fusion Suites Da Nang Beach Worth It?

The Fusion Suites Da Nang Beach concept — all-suite accommodation with spa treatments included in every booking — is genuinely unusual in the Da Nang market and delivers real value for guests who will use the spa. The calculation is straightforward: if you would pay for one or two spa treatments per stay anyway, the Fusion Suites rate becomes competitive or better than alternatives that charge separately for spa access. The suites are spacious, the beachfront location is strong, and the F&B is consistent. The main limitation is scale: the property is smaller and the pool is less dramatic than the major resort hotels. But for wellness-inclined travellers who want a boutique beachfront stay, the included spa element makes this a genuinely distinctive and worthwhile choice.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Fusion Suites Da Nang Beach, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — On-site. Direct beachfront on Da Nang's main coastal strip.
  • Dragon Bridge — 10 min taxi. Da Nang's fire-breathing icon — short taxi hop from the hotel.
  • My Khe Restaurant Strip — 5 min walk. Seafood grills, noodle houses, and beach cafes within walking distance.
  • Han River — 12 min taxi. Riverside promenade and evening dining scene.
  • Marble Mountains — 20 min taxi south. Classic half-day trip from any My Khe hotel.
  • Hoi An — 30 min by car. Essential Ancient Town day trip from Da Nang.
