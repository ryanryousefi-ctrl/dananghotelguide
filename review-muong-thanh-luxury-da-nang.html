Muong Thanh Luxury Da Nang Hotel Review

From $60/night ★ 8.1 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

Muong Thanh Luxury Da Nang Hotel is part of the Muong Thanh Group — Vietnam's largest domestic hotel chain with over 60 properties across the country. The Da Nang property on My Khe Beach represents the group's upscale 'Luxury' tier and delivers reliable four-star quality at some of the most competitive rates of any direct-beachfront hotel in the city.

The hotel is popular with domestic Vietnamese travellers, regional Asian visitors, and international budget-to-midscale tourists who want My Khe Beach access without the premium of international brand hotels. The Muong Thanh name carries strong recognition domestically and functions as a trust signal for Vietnamese travellers in particular.

Location

My Khe Beach, 5 km from Dragon Bridge and 7 km from the airport. The local restaurant strip is walkable, and the broader My Khe hotel zone provides a lively, active atmosphere. Hoi An is 30 minutes south.

  • Airport: 7 km / 12 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 12 min taxi
  • Beach restaurants: 5 min walk
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car

Rooms and Design

Rooms run 30–40 square metres with standard four-star furnishing. Design is functional contemporary — clean and well-maintained if somewhat generic. Ocean-view rooms on upper floors are the standout category. Balconies are present in most categories.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

An outdoor pool sits adjacent to the beach. Direct beach access on My Khe with sun lounger service. A fitness centre and basic spa are available.

Dining Options

A full breakfast buffet is available and strongly recommended — it is one of the better hotel breakfasts at this price point. An on-site restaurant serves Vietnamese and international cuisine. Dinner is more compellingly spent at the local seafood restaurants a short walk away.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $60–$90/night
  • Shoulder season: $85–$130/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $110–$185/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

Budget-to-midscale travellers who want My Khe Beach access at minimum cost, domestic Vietnamese visitors, and regional Asian tourists. Strong for shorter stays and those who plan to spend most of their time outside the hotel exploring Da Nang and Hoi An.

Pros
  • One of the best price-to-beach-location ratios on My Khe
  • Reliable domestic brand with consistent standards
  • Strong breakfast buffet for the price point
  • Direct beachfront access
  • Walkable to local restaurants
Cons
  • Design generic compared to boutique competitors
  • Service quality variable compared to international chains
  • Pool small compared to resort alternatives
  • Less polished than international brand hotels at similar rates

Final Verdict

Muong Thanh Luxury Da Nang Hotel is the smart value pick for travellers who want My Khe Beach access without paying premium rates. It is not the most exciting hotel in Da Nang, but it delivers the basics reliably and at a price that leaves budget for the local seafood restaurants and a day trip to Hoi An.

Is the Muong Thanh Luxury Da Nang Hotel Worth It?

Muong Thanh is Vietnam's largest domestic hotel chain, and the Da Nang Luxury property represents the brand's upper tier. It delivers a reasonable standard of comfort at rates that are competitive with international brands of similar positioning. The My Khe Beach location is a genuine asset. For domestic Vietnamese travellers and visitors who prioritise cost over brand prestige, it is a reliable choice. For international travellers comparing it to the Four Points, TMS Hotel, or Wyndham Soleil at similar price points, the Muong Thanh loses on room quality consistency and dining options, but wins on price. Overall: good value for budget-conscious travellers, a serviceable option for everyone else.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Muong Thanh Luxury Da Nang Hotel, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — On-site. Direct beach access on Da Nang's main coastal stretch.
  • Dragon Bridge — 10 min taxi. The city's landmark fire-breathing bridge.
  • My Khe Restaurant Strip — 5 min walk. Fresh seafood and local restaurants easily accessible on foot.
  • Han Market — 15 min taxi. Da Nang's main covered market.
  • Da Nang Airport — 8 km. Convenient connection for arrivals and departures.
  • Marble Mountains — 20 min taxi south. Half-day cultural excursion.
