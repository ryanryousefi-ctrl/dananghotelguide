Boutique Hotels in Da Nang

The best boutique hotels in Da Nang — intimate, design-led stays in An Thuong, My Khe, and Han River from $45/night.

Browse hotels →
Home › Hotels › Boutique Hotels

Da Nang's boutique scene centres on the An Thuong neighbourhood — a quiet grid of low-rise streets behind My Khe Beach, home to small-scale hotels with personality that the big resorts can't match. Expect locally designed rooms, rooftop terraces, and the kind of service where staff remember your coffee order. Prices run $45–$120/night, placing boutique stays firmly in mid-range territory. All five properties below have direct Booking.com listings with free cancellation options.

📍 Han River · riverside location

One of the few true boutique options on the Han River strip. River-facing rooms catch the nightly Dragon Bridge fire display — book early. Rooftop bar, solid Vietnamese breakfast buffet, and walking distance to the city's best street food.
