Why Da Nang for Boutique Stays

An Thuong sits one block from My Khe Beach — Da Nang's best boutique hotels concentrate here

Da Nang's boutique scene concentrates in An Thuong — a quiet grid of low-rise streets behind My Khe Beach that functions as the city's most liveable neighbourhood. The hotels here are small-scale by design: 30 to 80 rooms, local ownership or local design sensibility, rooftop terraces rather than resort pools, and staff who remember your coffee order by day two.

Prices run $45–$120 per night, placing boutique firmly in mid-range territory, and the value proposition is strong: you're trading resort pools and beach clubs for character, central location, and walking access to the best restaurant strip in the city. Most properties offer free cancellation and are bookable on Booking.com with real-time availability.

This guide covers five credible boutique properties — properties that actually deliver on the boutique promise of personal service and distinct design — with honest assessments of who each one suits. See also the complete Da Nang hotel guide for options across all budgets and categories.

◈ The An Thuong advantage

An Thuong sits 50–100m from My Khe Beach with a restaurant strip, coffee shops, and the city's best seafood market within a five-minute walk. Boutique hotels here cost 40–60% less than the beachfront five-stars while offering comparable access to the beach and neighbourhood life the big resorts can't provide.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best boutique hotel in Da Nang?
A La Carte Da Nang Beach is the strongest all-round boutique in the city. All-suite design, rooftop infinity pool overlooking My Khe Beach, and a lobby bar that functions as a genuine neighbourhood social hub. It sits 50 metres from the beach in An Thuong and has walking access to the best restaurant strip in Da Nang. From $75/night with free cancellation on most rates.
Where is the best area to stay for boutique hotels in Da Nang?
An Thuong is the premier boutique neighbourhood — a quiet grid of low-rise streets immediately behind My Khe Beach, one block from the sand and walking distance to Da Nang's best restaurants and coffee shops. The Han River district is the alternative for travellers who prioritise city character over beach access. See the full Where to Stay guide for a neighbourhood-by-neighbourhood breakdown.
How much do boutique hotels in Da Nang cost?
Da Nang boutique hotels typically run $45–$120 per night during mid-season (March–August). The ABBA Hotel is the entry point at around $48/night. A La Carte, TMS, and Wyndham Soleil range from $65–$85/night in the same period. Rates rise 20–30% during peak season (June–August) and during public holiday weekends. All five hotels in this guide have free cancellation options on most rates.
Is there a boutique hotel in Da Nang on the Han River?
Yes — Brilliant Hotel Da Nang is the strongest boutique option on the Han River. River-facing rooms overlook the Dragon Bridge, which fires on Friday and Saturday evenings. The hotel has a rooftop bar with 180° river panorama and is within walking distance of Bach Dang Walkway and the city's best street food. The beach is 10 minutes by Grab.
