Radisson Blu Resort Danang Review

From $150/night ★ 8.6 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

The Radisson Blu Resort Danang is a 372-room five-star beach resort on My Khe Beach, operated by Radisson Hotel Group. It occupies a substantial beachfront plot and offers the full range of five-star resort amenities — multiple pools, a spa, multiple restaurants, beach service, and Radisson Rewards loyalty integration. The resort is popular with European and Australian visitors who appreciate the brand's reliability.

Within the Da Nang five-star resort landscape, the Radisson Blu sits alongside the Sheraton Grand and Pullman as a dependable, brand-managed upper-upscale option on the My Khe strip. It offers slightly more rooms than its neighbours, which means a full range of categories is typically available even during peak season.

Location

My Khe Beach position, 7 km from Dragon Bridge and 9 km from the airport. The local restaurant strip is accessible on foot. Hoi An is 30 minutes south.

  • Airport: 9 km / 14 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 15 min taxi
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car
  • Beach restaurants: 5 min walk

Rooms and Design

372 rooms from around 40 square metres for entry Superior rooms to 250 square metres for the Presidential Suite. Design is polished contemporary — not as distinctive as Naman or Melia, but well-maintained and consistent with five-star expectations. Ocean-view rooms on upper floors are the most popular category.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

A large resort pool with children's section, a smaller adults pool, and a rooftop pool. Direct beach access on My Khe with full service. A full spa, fitness centre, kids' club, and tennis courts complete the facilities.

Dining Options

The Latitude 16 restaurant is the main all-day dining venue with a strong breakfast buffet. A beach grill and poolside bar operate through the day. A rooftop bar offers cocktails and city views. Food quality is solid, and the breakfast is one of the better hotel spreads on the My Khe strip.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $150–$200/night
  • Shoulder season: $200–$290/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $270–$420/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

International leisure travellers seeking reliable five-star quality, Radisson Rewards members, couples wanting a beach holiday, and guests who want the full resort package without the premium of Da Nang's top-tier properties.

Pros
  • Reliable five-star resort standards
  • Large resort pool with multiple areas
  • Direct My Khe beachfront access
  • Strong breakfast buffet
  • Radisson Rewards points earned
  • 372 rooms means availability usually good
Cons
  • Design less distinctive than boutique competitors
  • Resort scale can feel impersonal
  • On-site dining adequate but not destination-worthy
  • 15 min taxi to Da Nang city centre

Final Verdict

The Radisson Blu Resort Danang is a well-executed, reliably good five-star beach resort. It does not set out to be the most memorable hotel in Da Nang, but it delivers consistent quality, strong pool facilities, and genuine beachfront access at rates that are competitive within the five-star segment. For Radisson Rewards members and international leisure travellers who want predictable luxury, it is a safe and satisfying choice.

Is the Radisson Blu Resort Danang Worth It?

The Radisson Blu Resort Danang is one of the larger and more family-focused properties on the My Khe strip, with an expansive water park that makes it stand out from every other hotel in this guide. For families with children, the water park alone elevates it into a top-three family hotel in Da Nang — no other beach resort on My Khe offers anything comparable. For couples and solo travellers, the calculus is different: you are paying partially for a family resort infrastructure you may not use. The beach access and rooms are consistently strong, and the Radisson brand delivers reliable service standards. If you are travelling with children aged 4–14, this is likely the best pure resort value in Da Nang.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Radisson Blu Resort Danang, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — On-site. Direct beachfront — broad sand and calm water in the dry season.
  • Dragon Bridge — 15 min taxi. Da Nang's iconic bridge and fire show on Friday and Saturday nights.
  • Han River — 15 min taxi. City-centre riverfront with restaurants and rotating bridge.
  • Marble Mountains — 20 min taxi south. Five marble and limestone hills — a half-day cultural excursion.
  • My Khe Restaurant Strip — 5 min walk. Fresh seafood and local Vietnamese restaurants within walking distance.
  • Hoi An — 30 min by car. The essential Central Vietnam day trip from Da Nang.
