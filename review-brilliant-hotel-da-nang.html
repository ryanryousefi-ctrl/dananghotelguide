Brilliant Hotel Da Nang Review

From $55/night ★ 8.4 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

The Brilliant Hotel Da Nang is a consistently highly-rated four-star hotel on the Han River, directly adjacent to Dragon Bridge. It has developed a strong following among travellers who want a well-priced city-centre base with strong views and a genuinely included breakfast — without the expense of a five-star resort. On Booking.com and TripAdvisor, the Brilliant regularly appears in 'best value' and 'top-rated' lists for Da Nang, which reflects its combination of location, included amenities, and competitive pricing.

The hotel is locally owned and operated, which shows in the warmth of the service culture and the personal attention that a smaller, independently run property can provide relative to international chain hotels.

Location

The Han River position is the hotel's defining advantage. Dragon Bridge — Da Nang's most iconic landmark, which breathes fire on Friday and Saturday evenings — is literally next door. From the rooftop pool, the bridge and city panorama are visible in their full glory. Han Market is a 5-minute walk. My Khe Beach is a 10-minute taxi ride.

  • Airport: 4 km / 8 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: adjacent
  • Han Market: 5 min walk
  • My Khe Beach: 10 min taxi
  • Hoi An: 30 min by car

Rooms and Design

Rooms run 28–42 square metres. Decor is clean and contemporary — not the most design-forward hotel in Da Nang, but well-maintained. River-facing rooms on higher floors offer excellent Dragon Bridge views, especially on weekend evenings when the bridge performs. Breakfast is included in all standard rates.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

The rooftop pool is small but positioned for maximum visual impact — overlooking Dragon Bridge and the Han River curve. It is one of the most photographed hotel pool positions in Da Nang. A spa and fitness centre are available.

Dining Options

Breakfast is included and consistently well-reviewed — a wide spread of Vietnamese and international options. The rooftop bar is popular in the evenings for Dragon Bridge viewing. On-site dinner options are limited and the surrounding streets more than compensate.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $55–$80/night (incl. breakfast)
  • Shoulder season: $75–$115/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $95–$170/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

Budget-to-midscale travellers who want a central city location, Dragon Bridge views, and an included breakfast at a price point well below the Novotel Premier Han River. Solo travellers, couples on shorter city breaks, and food-focused visitors who want to eat out for most meals.

Pros
  • Adjacent to Dragon Bridge — best location for fire show viewing
  • Rooftop pool overlooking the Han River
  • Breakfast included at most rates — genuine value
  • Competitive rates for a well-located four-star hotel
  • Locally owned with warm service culture
  • 4 km from airport
Cons
  • Smaller rooms than resort alternatives
  • 5 km from My Khe Beach — no resort atmosphere
  • Pool is compact — not for lap swimming
  • Street noise from river road on lower floors

Final Verdict

The Brilliant Hotel Da Nang offers one of the city's best combinations of location, value, and included amenities. The Dragon Bridge position is irreplaceable, the breakfast is genuinely good, and the rooftop pool view is one of the most memorable in Da Nang at any price point. For city-focused travellers on a sensible budget, it is a near-perfect choice.

Is Brilliant Hotel Da Nang Worth It?

Brilliant Hotel Da Nang is a long-established city-centre hotel that has maintained its position through consistent service and a strong Han River location. For travellers who want a traditional hotel experience in Da Nang's city core — rather than a beach resort or modern luxury property — it remains a reliable choice. It does not compete on design with the Grand Mercure or Silk Path Grand, and the facilities are more limited. But the Han River views, the competitive pricing, and the hotel's genuine longevity in the Da Nang market give it a dependable character. For budget-to-midscale city travellers, it earns its reputation.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Brilliant Hotel Da Nang, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • Han River Waterfront — On-site. Directly on the river with views toward Dragon Bridge.
  • Dragon Bridge — 10 min walk. One of Da Nang's best walking-distance landmark access points.
  • Han Market — 15 min walk. The city's main market for local food, produce, and souvenirs.
  • Da Nang Cathedral — 15 min walk. The famous pink church — a short stroll from the hotel.
  • My Khe Beach — 10 min taxi. The main beach is an easy and inexpensive taxi ride.
  • Museum of Cham Sculpture — 15 min walk. The world's finest collection of Cham artefacts — free or low entry fee.
