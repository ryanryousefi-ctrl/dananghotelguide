Pullman Danang Beach Resort Review

From $145/night ★ 8.5 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

The Pullman Danang Beach Resort is part of Accor's upscale Pullman brand, occupying a beachfront site on My Khe Beach. The 187-room resort opened in 2014 and has established itself as a dependable mid-to-upper luxury option in the Da Nang market — not the most architecturally spectacular property, but one that delivers consistently across pool facilities, room quality, and service. Accor ALL loyalty members can earn and redeem points here.

The Pullman's positioning is slightly more business-and-leisure friendly than the purely leisure-focused resorts further south. A well-equipped business centre and meeting rooms, combined with the resort amenities, make it popular with corporate groups and bleisure travellers.

Location

My Khe Beach in front of the resort is clean and wide. The location is approximately 8 km from Dragon Bridge and 3 km from the Hilton Da Nang. The local restaurant scene on the beach road is accessible on foot, and the airport is a 15-minute taxi ride. Hoi An can be reached in around 35 minutes by car.

  • Airport: 10 km / 15 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 18 min taxi
  • Hoi An: 35 min by car
  • My Khe restaurants: 5–10 min walk

Rooms and Design

The 187 rooms and suites are contemporary in design, with sea or garden views, balconies, and warm neutral tones. Superior rooms run around 40 square metres; Deluxe Ocean rooms are around 50 sqm with better views. The design is polished without being particularly distinctive — functional luxury rather than architectural showmanship.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

The main outdoor pool is well-sized and sits between the hotel building and the beach, offering good views. A smaller indoor pool is available. The beach is equipped with umbrellas and sun beds for guests. The Pullman Spa has a full treatment menu and is consistently well-reviewed. A fitness centre, tennis court, and bicycle rental are also available.

Dining Options

The Edge Restaurant serves international and Vietnamese cuisine for all-day dining, and the breakfast buffet is included in many rate plans — a genuine advantage. A poolside bar and beach bar operate through the day. The restaurant quality is solid, though the beach road's local seafood options are worth exploring for dinner.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $145–$195/night
  • Shoulder season: $195–$270/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $250–$380/night

Who This Hotel Is Best For

The Pullman suits Accor ALL loyalty members, bleisure travellers who want resort facilities alongside meeting infrastructure, and couples seeking a dependable beachfront hotel without the premium of smaller boutique properties. It is a strong all-rounder but lacks the distinctive character of Da Nang's most memorable properties.

Pros
  • Breakfast typically included in room rates
  • Accor ALL loyalty points earned
  • Direct My Khe beachfront access
  • Good meeting and business facilities
  • Reliable five-star service standards
  • Walkable to local restaurant strip
Cons
  • Design lacks the personality of top boutique competitors
  • Pool area can get crowded during peak months
  • Less distinctive than pricier competitors
  • Limited nightlife or entertainment on-site

Final Verdict

The Pullman Danang Beach Resort is a reliable, well-managed beachfront hotel that ticks most of the boxes for a quality Da Nang beach holiday. It is not the most exciting property in the city, but it delivers consistent quality and the Accor loyalty benefits make it a sensible choice for programme members. When breakfast is included in the rate, the value proposition is particularly strong.

Is the Pullman Danang Beach Resort Worth It?

The Pullman Danang Beach Resort is a good choice for beachfront accommodation with strong design credentials, but it occupies a mid-range luxury position rather than a true five-star resort tier. The beach access is excellent, the pool is well-designed, and the rooms are contemporary and comfortable. Where it falls slightly short is in dining depth and service consistency compared to the Hyatt Regency or Sheraton Grand at similar or slightly higher rates. For Accor loyalty members and travellers who want a modern beach resort aesthetic with My Khe frontage, it is a solid pick. Travellers prioritising the best overall resort value should compare it closely against the Sheraton Grand before booking.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Pullman Danang Beach Resort, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — On-site. Direct access to Da Nang's main beach — broad sand and calm seas in dry season.
  • Dragon Bridge — 15 min taxi. Da Nang's iconic fire-breathing dragon bridge.
  • Han River — 15 min taxi. City-centre riverside with restaurants, bars, and the rotating Love Bridge.
  • My Khe Restaurant Strip — 5 min walk. Dozens of fresh seafood restaurants along the beachfront road.
  • Marble Mountains — 20 min taxi. Five marble hills with pagodas, caves, and panoramic city views.
  • Hoi An — 30 min by car. UNESCO Heritage town — most popular day trip from Da Nang.
