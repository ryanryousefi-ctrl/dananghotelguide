Da Nang Marriott Resort & Spa, Non Nuoc Beach Villas Review

From $280/night ★ 9.2 Booking.com Reviewed 2026

Overview

The Da Nang Marriott Resort & Spa, Non Nuoc Beach Villas is Marriott International's flagship Da Nang property and one of the standout luxury villa resorts in central Vietnam. Opened in 2020, the resort comprises 200 villas, each with a private pool, across a substantial Non Nuoc beachfront plot. The Marriott brand brings its five-star service infrastructure — strong Bonvoy loyalty integration, high room quality standards, and a well-trained service culture — to what is a genuinely impressive physical product.

The resort competes directly with the nearby Hyatt Regency and Melia for the same high-end leisure traveller, but its all-villa format gives it a distinctive character. For Marriott Bonvoy members, it represents one of the most compelling points redemption opportunities in Da Nang.

Location

Non Nuoc Beach is quieter and generally cleaner than the busy My Khe strip. The Marble Mountains are visible from many of the villas, providing a distinctive inland backdrop. At 13 km from the city, the resort is genuinely in resort-mode distance from Da Nang's urban energy. Hoi An is 22 km south — about 25 minutes by car, making day trips straightforward.

  • Airport: 15 km / 20 min taxi
  • Dragon Bridge: 25 min taxi
  • Marble Mountains: 5 min taxi
  • Hoi An: 22 km / 25 min by car
  • Non Nuoc Beach: direct access

Rooms and Design

All 200 villas have private pools, with sizes ranging from 100 to 500 square metres for the largest presidential options. The design language is contemporary Vietnamese — white plastered walls, dark timber accents, ceramic tiles, and generous use of indoor-outdoor space. Larger villas have fully equipped kitchens and multiple bedrooms. The Marriott design team has achieved a balance between brand consistency and locally inspired character.

Pools, Beach, and Amenities

Every villa has a private infinity pool. A central resort pool is also available for those who want a social swim environment. The beach is directly accessible via the resort grounds. A full-service Quan Spa, a well-equipped fitness centre, tennis courts, cycling, watersports, and a kids' club are all part of the resort offering.

Dining Options

Three dining outlets cover Vietnamese street food-inspired casual dining, international buffet, and a beachside bar. The Street Food Kitchen is a popular informal dining option. In-villa dining is available throughout the day. Breakfast quality is consistently well-reviewed.

Typical Price Range

  • Low season (May–August): $280–$380/night for 1-bed villa
  • Shoulder season: $380–$520/night
  • Peak season (December–February): $500–$800+/night
  • Marriott Bonvoy redemptions available

Who This Hotel Is Best For

Marriott Bonvoy members looking to redeem points, couples and families wanting private pool villas with five-star service, and guests who want the Marriott service culture in a villa format rather than a conventional hotel room.

Pros
  • All-villa resort with every unit having a private pool
  • Strong Marriott Bonvoy redemption opportunity
  • Five-star service consistency across the property
  • Quiet Non Nuoc Beach with more seclusion than My Khe
  • Proximity to Marble Mountains for excursions
  • Well-designed villas with strong indoor-outdoor living
Cons
  • High nightly rates — particularly in peak season
  • 13 km from city centre — taxis required
  • All-villa format means no 'entry-level' room option
  • Peak-season availability fills rapidly

Final Verdict

The Da Nang Marriott Resort & Spa Non Nuoc Beach Villas is one of the finest hotel products in Da Nang. The combination of private pool villas, Marriott's five-star service culture, and a quieter Non Nuoc Beach setting creates an experience that justifies the premium rates. For Bonvoy members it is an exceptional redemption. For those paying cash, it competes very seriously with the nearby Premier Village for the title of Da Nang's best villa resort.

Is the Marriott Resort Da Nang Worth It?

The Marriott Resort Da Nang is one of the newer luxury additions to the My Khe Beach strip and delivers on most fronts. The design is fresh, the pool is well-executed, and the beach access is direct. For Marriott Bonvoy members, the points earning at a resort of this standard makes it particularly appealing. The main competitive challenge is that Da Nang's upper-midscale and luxury beach resort segment is crowded — the Sheraton Grand, Hyatt Regency, and Pullman all compete in overlapping territory. The Marriott wins on brand newness and room quality; where it loses is dining, which is competent but not distinctive. For a reliable luxury beachfront stay with Bonvoy benefits, it is worth it.

Nearby Attractions & Getting Around

One of Da Nang's advantages as a destination is how compact it is — most major attractions are within 20–30 minutes of any hotel in the city. From Marriott Resort Da Nang, key landmarks and beaches are easy to reach by taxi or GrabCar, and both services are reliable, metered, and inexpensive by international standards.

  • My Khe Beach — On-site. Direct beachfront on Da Nang's premier ocean stretch.
  • Dragon Bridge — 15 min taxi. Da Nang's most iconic structure — weekend fire show not to be missed.
  • Han River Dining Strip — 15 min taxi. Riverside restaurants and the famous rotating bridge.
  • Marble Mountains — 20 min taxi south. Five limestone formations with Buddhist temples — a half-day excursion.
  • Da Nang Museum of Cham Sculpture — 15 min taxi. The world's largest collection of Cham artefacts.
  • Hoi An Ancient Town — 30 min by car. Must-do day trip from any Da Nang hotel.
