How to Get to Ba Na Hills

What to Actually Do at Ba Na Hills

Is Ba Na Hills Worth It?

This depends entirely on who you are and what you expect. Ba Na Hills is a well-operated, substantial attraction â€” the infrastructure is good, the cable car is an impressive engineering achievement, and the Golden Bridge is genuinely worth photographing. The theme-park elements are high quality by regional standards.

But the ticket price is steep relative to Da Nang's other attractions (the Marble Mountains cost 150,000 VND; Hoi An is 120,000 VND), the crowds at peak periods are serious, and the overall character of the attraction is commercial. It is a managed experience, not an authentic Vietnamese cultural site.
